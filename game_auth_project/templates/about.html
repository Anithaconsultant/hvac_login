About Us

What is this?

Phantom Load is an interactive 3D computer game set in a realistic building environment. It will have 3 levels of play:

Level 1: Building Audit Challenge
Level 2: Troubleshooting Challenge
Level 3: Performance Optimization Challenge

Players explore the building, identify its components and energy use, investigate systems, improve the building performance, and lower carbon emissions.

The current version is a live demo, a pre-alpha version of the game.

What is a phantom load?

Phantom loads are the electricity silently consumed when devices are plugged in but not actively used. It is the power used for standby functions, remote capabilities, and instant-on features even when appearing "off." Broadly, energy use that does not lead to productive use can be considered a phantom load.

Join the hunt to discover phantom loads and use practical strategies to combat this energy waste.

Why this game?

The game transforms how we understand building science and energy flows by making the invisible visible. This hands-on approach creates a fundamentally different learning experience than typical educational settings, where building systems are taught as theoretical concepts.
